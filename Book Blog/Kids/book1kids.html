The Hare and the Tortoise

by Mairi Mackinnon

Genre: Adventure, Survival Fiction

Published: 1987

Rating: ★★★★☆ (4.5/5)

Synopsis:

The Hare and the Tortoise is a classic fable about a speedy hare who brags about how fast he is. The slow tortoise challenges him to a race. The hare races ahead but gets lazy and takes a nap, while the tortoise keeps moving steadily. In the end, the tortoise wins because he never gave up!

Why Read It?

This story teaches a powerful lesson: "Slow and steady wins the race." It’s great for kids (and adults!) because it shows that hard work and patience matter more than just natural talent or speed. The hare’s overconfidence makes him lose, while the tortoise’s determination leads to success. It’s a short, fun tale with a message that stays useful for life!
